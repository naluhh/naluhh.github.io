Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Phone number (used for account creation and authentication via one-time passcode)

  • Message content and other communications you send through the Service
  • Usage Data

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. Usage Data may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address, device type, mobile device unique identifiers, mobile operating system, the type of mobile internet browser you use, the features of our Service that you use, the time and date of your use, the time spent on those features, and other diagnostic data.

Information Collected while Using the Application

Contacts

With your permission, the App accesses the contacts stored on your device to enable contact-join notifications and to help you find people you know who also use Fenn. We do not upload, store, or transmit the phone numbers, names, or other contact details from your address book in plaintext.

Instead, phone numbers from your contacts are converted on your device into salted cryptographic hashes, which are transmitted to our servers solely to check for matches against other Fenn users. We do not retain hashes that do not match a Fenn user, and we do not use this data for advertising, marketing, or sale to third parties.

You can revoke contacts access at any time through your device settings.

Location

With your permission, the App collects your device's location, including in the background if you grant that permission, to provide core coordination features such as arrival and departure detection and presence signals shared with people you have explicitly chosen to share with.

Location data is processed to derive human-readable status signals (for example, "at home" or "on the way") and is shared only with the specific users you have authorized to receive it. We do not sell your location data or use it for advertising.

You can revoke or modify location access at any time through your device settings.

Calendar

With your permission, the App accesses your device calendar to detect scheduling intent in conversations and assist with coordination among the people you communicate with through the Service. Calendar event data is processed to identify potential events to coordinate around and to surface relevant suggestions within the App.

You can revoke calendar access at any time through your device settings.

Photos and Camera

With your permission, the App may access your device's camera and photo library so that you can share images within conversations and customize your profile. This information may be uploaded to the Company's servers or to a Service Provider's server, or it may be stored only on your device, depending on how you use the feature.

You can revoke camera and photo library access at any time through your device settings.

Notifications

With your permission, the App sends push notifications to inform you of activity relevant to your account, such as new messages, contact-join events, and coordination updates. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

AI Processing

The Service uses AI models to analyze conversation content, calendar events, and location patterns in order to surface coordination suggestions and power core features of the App.

To provide these features, message content and related context may be transmitted to third-party AI model providers acting as our Service Providers under contractual confidentiality and data-handling obligations. These providers do not use your data to train their models. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.
