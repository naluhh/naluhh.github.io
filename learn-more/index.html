Fair questions.

Things people ask before letting an AI into the group chat.

So... another messaging app?

Fenn
You don't need another messaging app. You need one that works for you.
Yes, your group downloads me — free, takes about a minute. But you're not moving your life over. Bring one thread that never gets anywhere: the trip, the carpool, the dinner that's been "soon" since March. I'll take it from there.

Are you reading everything we say?

Fenn
Yes — that's how I work. I can't remind you about the net if I don't know about the net.
But no human ever reads your conversations. Nothing you say is shared with anyone, and nothing is ever sold. It stays between us.

Will you be annoying?

Fenn
I speak when spoken to, or when something actually needs doing — a reminder, a decision, a heads-up. No streaks, no confetti, no "just checking in."

What does it cost?

Fenn
Free for group chats. A paid plan with longer memory is coming for the heavily organized; the free one stays genuinely useful.

What can I use it on?

Fenn
iPhone today, Android on the way. Free either way, and setup takes about a minute.
