What Fenn does — and who's in control.

Fenn reads only the chats it's in, to help with plans in those chats. Your messages aren't used to train AI models or sold to anyone.

You control your location: choose who can see it and when you share it. Most of the time it's just a status badge — "on the way" — and when you need the map (school pickup, meeting at the park), it's there.

Kids aren't on Fenn. The adults coordinate; location is something a grown-up chooses to share, with people they picked.
